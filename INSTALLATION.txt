**1)** Extract/Unzip the hud

**2)** Choose between one of the 2 versions, Centered or Lowered.

**3)** Copy the desired EJP Hud folder (**NOT** EJP-Hud-Master, either EJP Hud Centered or EJP Hud Lowered)

**4)** Paste The folder to your custom tf folder (C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\SteamApps\common\Team Fortress 2\tf\custom\EJP Hud Centered)
